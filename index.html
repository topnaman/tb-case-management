Methodology Note

Contact investigations have two separate workload components that add together:

CI administrative weight — case review, planning, coordination, and documentation; charged once per investigation regardless of how many contacts are identified.

Individual contact weight — screening, testing, follow-up, and counseling per contact person. Total contact workload = (CIs by DPHNs) × (contacts per CI) × (contact weight).
